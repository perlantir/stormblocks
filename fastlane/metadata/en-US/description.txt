Place blocks. Clear lines. Rescue survivors. Push back the storm.

Storm Blocks is a premium casual 3D block puzzle with a rescue twist. Drag colorful toy-like pieces onto an 8x8 board, complete rows and columns, rescue tiny survivors, and trigger Storm Pushback to blast the living blue-purple storm away from your warm central camp.

Play the way you want:

Endless Storm - chase a high score as the storm keeps pressing in.
Daily Storm - beat the same deterministic challenge as everyone else today.
Storm Trail - clear a long map of handcrafted-style puzzle challenges.
Tempest Trials - take on a weekly set of storm runs.
Practice Mode - relax with lower pressure and no leaderboard.

Storm Blocks is built around fair play and satisfying puzzle mastery. Progression is cosmetic-only. There are no forced ads, paid power, paid speedups, gacha, or loot boxes.
