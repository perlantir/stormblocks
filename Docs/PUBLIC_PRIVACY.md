# Storm Blocks Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-05.

Storm Blocks is designed as a free-first casual puzzle game with no forced ads, no paid power, no paid speedups, no gacha, and no loot boxes.

## Data We Store On Device

Storm Blocks stores gameplay and settings data locally on your device:

- Best scores and run history.
- Daily Storm and Tempest Trials history.
- Storm Trail progress.
- Cosmetic unlocks and equipped cosmetic choices.
- Accessibility and settings preferences.

## Game Center

If you use Game Center, Apple may process your Game Center profile, leaderboard scores, achievements, and related account information under Apple's Game Center and Apple ID policies.

## Sharing

If you choose to share a result, Storm Blocks creates a local share card and opens the iOS share sheet. You choose where the share card goes.

## Advertising, Tracking, And Purchases

Storm Blocks currently includes:

- No third-party advertising SDKs.
- No tracking SDKs.
- No App Tracking Transparency prompt.
- No in-app purchases.
- No loot boxes or gacha systems.

## Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact support@perlantir.com.
